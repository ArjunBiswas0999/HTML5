Share Component

Introduction

A Share Component is a ui5 component that applications can use to share information as a group feed entry to JAM.

The Share Component contains other ui5 controls that enables a user to enter/edit the information shared to JAM.

The information needed for the share operation are:

  • The Jam group: the user can choose the group from the DDB rendered inside the component
  • The content of the group's feed entry; this is composed of two parts:
    • Note: this comes from the note text area that is rendered inside the component
    • ID: is an hidden ID (probably a URL) passed from the application, and sent to Jam
